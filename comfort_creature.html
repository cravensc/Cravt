Comfort Creature

Lover, please stay under the duvet.
It’s too cold out there and too soft in here.
There are many minutes in the day
And I’m the only one that really needs you anyway.

Amazing you are, leading me astray
And I offered nothing but bedded bliss.
Let me join you in this luxurious bath
And admire your effort to be up at last.

Anyone will tell you how great you are
And that one day your dreams will come true.
Surely, while you’re doing derivatives or taking out the trash
They will arrive just for you.

Anxiety and fear need not make you tear.
Let’s call the sweet slayer of loathe and doubt.
She can replace hate with love and help us find rest
So we may never need to bear the weight of success.
